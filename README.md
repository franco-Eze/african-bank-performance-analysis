📊 African Bank Financial Performance Analysis (2018–2023)

📌 Overview

This project analyzes the financial performance of selected African banks from 2018 to 2023 using key metrics such as revenue, net profit, total assets, and efficiency ratios (ROA & ROE).

⸻

🎯 Objectives
	•	Analyze revenue and profitability trends
	•	Compare financial performance across banks
	•	Evaluate efficiency using ROA and ROE
	•	Identify growth patterns and performance gaps

⸻

🛠️ Tools Used
	•	Google Sheets
	•	Excel
	•	Data Visualization (Charts)

⸻

📈 Key Insights

🔹 Profitability
	•	Net profit increased across most banks after 2021
	•	Commercial International Bank leads in profit growth

🔹 ROA (Efficiency)
	•	Decline observed around 2020, followed by recovery
	•	Equity Bank shows strong asset utilization

🔹 ROE (Returns)
	•	Commercial International Bank maintains highest returns
	•	Standard Bank remains more stable but lower

🔹 Overall
	•	Growth trend is consistent across banks
	•	Efficiency varies significantly between institutions
